WELCOME

I consider myself a creative artist and then a photographer striving to create works of art. My passion is in creation…envisioning something in my mind then working at it till it becomes a reality. My artwork focuses on black and white photography with compositions containing architectural elements, abstracts of objects or places, and urban scenes. Please feel free to contact me with any questions or to purchase personally made archival quality prints. Thank you for stoping by…explore and enjoy!
